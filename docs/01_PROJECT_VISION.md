# TaxVault – Project Vision

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Locked (Pending Approval)

---

# 1. Vision

TaxVault is a Client Document Collection & Tracking Platform built specifically for Chartered Accountants to simplify the collection, organization, tracking, and management of income tax documents.

Instead of spending time requesting documents multiple times, searching through emails, WhatsApp chats, downloaded folders, and physical files, TaxVault provides a single platform that manages the complete document collection lifecycle.

The objective is to ensure that every client's documents are complete, organized, and ready before the Income Tax Return (ITR) preparation begins.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Every tax season, Chartered Accountants spend a significant amount of time on administrative activities instead of tax preparation.

Typical challenges include:

- Requesting documents from clients repeatedly.
- Receiving documents through multiple communication channels.
- Downloading and manually organizing files.
- Renaming and moving documents into folders.
- Tracking missing documents using spreadsheets or memory.
- Following up with clients multiple times.
- Determining whether a client is ready for ITR filing.

As the number of clients increases, these manual processes become difficult to manage, leading to delays, duplicate work, and increased operational effort.

---

# 3. Our Vision

TaxVault aims to become the central platform where every client document is collected, organized, tracked, and made available for tax preparation.

The platform removes the administrative burden of document management, allowing Chartered Accountants to focus on preparing accurate tax returns instead of managing files.

---

# 4. Product Philosophy

TaxVault is built around one simple principle:

> **Request Documents → Receive Documents → Organize Documents → Track Progress → Ready for ITR**

Everything in the application supports this workflow.

---

# 5. Core Principles

## 5.1 CA-Centric Design

The product is designed around the daily workflow of Chartered Accountants rather than forcing them to adopt a new way of working.

---

## 5.2 Bulk First

Most firms already maintain client information in Excel or existing software.

TaxVault therefore treats Bulk Client Import as the primary onboarding mechanism.

Individual client creation remains available for new clients.

---

## 5.3 Channel Agnostic Document Submission

Clients may submit documents using any supported communication channel.

Regardless of how documents arrive, TaxVault processes them through a unified document intake workflow.

The source of the document should never affect how it is identified, organized, stored, or tracked.

---

## 5.4 One Digital Vault per Client

Every client has a dedicated Digital Vault where all tax-related documents are securely organized and maintained.

---

## 5.5 Document Collection Before Tax Preparation

TaxVault focuses on ensuring document completeness before tax preparation begins.

The product's responsibility ends when the client is ready for ITR preparation.

---

# 6. Primary Users

The primary users of TaxVault are:

- Chartered Accountants
- CA Firms
- Tax Consultants
- Office Staff responsible for document collection and client communication

Clients interact with the platform only for submitting requested documents.

# 8. Success Criteria

TaxVault is considered successful when it enables a Chartered Accountant to:

- Import existing client data in minutes.
- Request documents from hundreds of clients with minimal effort.
- Receive documents without manual organization.
- Instantly identify missing documents.
- Know which clients are ready for tax preparation.
- Eliminate repetitive document follow-ups and manual file management.

---

# 9. Scope

TaxVault is not an Income Tax Return filing application.

Its responsibility is to manage the complete client document collection and tracking process, ensuring every client reaches the "Ready for ITR" stage efficiently and consistently.

---
